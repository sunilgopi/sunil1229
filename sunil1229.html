Jr NTR, also known as Nandamuri Taraka Rama Rao Jr., is a prominent Indian actor known for his work in Telugu cinema. Born on May 20, 1983, he is the grandson of the legendary actor and former Chief Minister of Andhra Pradesh, N. T. Rama Rao. Jr NTR made his acting debut with the film "Ninnu Choodalani" in 2001 and quickly rose to fame with his versatile performances and charismatic screen presence. He has starred in numerous successful films, earning accolades for his roles in movies like "Simhadri," "Yamadonga," and "Janatha Garage." Apart from acting, Jr NTR is also known for his exceptional dancing skills and philanthropic activities.
